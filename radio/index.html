Listen via SDR. Each AM station shows its 5 nearest public KiwiSDR receivers (by distance from the transmitter), each opening that KiwiSDR pre-tuned to the frequency in AM mode (/?f={freq}am). The receiver list is pulled fresh each time the page is rebuilt. FM stations have no SDR option — KiwiSDRs only cover ~0–30 MHz and cannot receive Japanese FM (76–95 MHz).
Before you tune in. KiwiSDR slots are limited and most receivers enforce a per-user time limit, so you may be disconnected or find a receiver full. Most KiwiSDRs also publicly list the approximate location of connected listeners — using a VPN is highly recommended.
